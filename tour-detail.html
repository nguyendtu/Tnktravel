Bach Ma National Park Trekking 2 days

TOUR INFORMATION

  • Tour Code: VCTT-01

  • Tour Length: 2 days / 1 night

  • Tour Type: Trekking Kayaking and Cycling

  • Start From: Hue

  • Review: Be the first to review this tour

Located at the south of Hue city, Bach Ma National Park covers an area of 22,031 ha. With steep mountains and dense tropical and sub-tropical forests, this area is home to a wide variety of animals and plants from both the northern and southern areas of the country, making Bach Ma one of the recognized centers of bio-diversity in Indochina. A two-day trip to Bach Ma National Park offers a delightful opportunity to relax and discover the nature.
