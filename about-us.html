About us

In 10 years of operation, our company has grown from a small team of 6 to a staff of over 80. This includes the management board, sales and marketing and operations departments. We also provide a professional, reliable and safe transport fleet.

Our clients

Over the past 10 years, we have served more than 1.300.000 travellers from Japan (18 %), Australia (30%), North America (15%), France (8% ), Germany (5%), Italy (7%) and the rest from other European countries. We have also served a lot of Muslim travellers from around the world. Our Muslim markets are from Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, South Africa, and countries in the Middle East as well.

Our products

  • - TNK Travel is one of the leading tour operators for S.I.C tours to major destinations in Vietnam. Everyday, we organize trips for more than 200 tourists to the key tourist locations of Cu Chi tunnels, the Mekong delta area, the DMZ tour in Hue, Ha Long bay cruises and Sa Pa. We use our own transportation and expert local guides.

  • - TNK Travel has been online since 2003 and is a leading travel website portal providing reliable travel solutions for vacation planners who prefer to deal directly with a locally based tour operator. We specialize in private family tours and tailor-made tours for individuals, families or groups to the Indochina peninsula which includes Vietnam, Laos and Cambodia.

  • - TNK Travel is the main share holder of the Beautiful Saigon Hotel Group and Saigon Hostel Group and through them we can provide a comfortable and welcoming stay for travellers to Ho Chi Minh City, at an affordable price.

  • - TNK Travel has wholesale contracts with hoteliers, cruise companies and airlines and therefore we can obtain unbeatable rates for our clients compared to other travel agencies.

  • - TNK Japan has12 specialists dedicated to assisting Japanese clients as Vietnam has seen a greatly increasing number of Japanese tourists over the last few years.
